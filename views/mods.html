Mods

Runtime modifications made easy

Your particular view of some feature might collide with what the rest of the users expect. For example, you might expect that a click on the text label of an item should un/check that item, but another user might expect that same action to trigger a open/close.

Sure the directive could be bloated with options, but I don't think that is the best way to go, so instead I created 'mods' (v6.3.x). A 'mod' is a file that modifies the behavior of this directive at runtime, without any extra configuration required.

Currently the available mods are:

  • toggle-checked-state-on-label-click - Will trigger a un/check when a click is performed on a item's text label.
  • toggle-open-state-on-label-click - Will trigger a open/close when a click is performed on a item's text label.
  • toggle-open-state-nodes-checked-state-leafs-on-label-click - Will trigger a open/close or un/check when a click is performed on a node's or on a leaf's text label, respectively.

Once again, the only thing you should do is include, after the directive, one (or more) of those mods in your HTML: 

			
<script src="/dist/prod/mods/toggle-open-state-on-label-click.min.js"></script>
